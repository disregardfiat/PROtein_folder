# Government use & license

The project is released under the **MIT License** with an **additional government-use restriction**. The full legal text is in the [`LICENSE`](https://github.com/disregardfiat/protein_folder/blob/main/LICENSE) file in the repository.

A plain-language summary is in [`GOVERNMENT_USE.md`](https://github.com/disregardfiat/protein_folder/blob/main/GOVERNMENT_USE.md).

PyPI metadata (`pyproject.toml`) includes this restriction in the **project description** so automated tooling and procurement reviews can surface it early.

**Individuals, companies, universities, and non-profits** retain normal MIT freedoms (subject to the notice requirement). **Government entities** need an explicit paid **Government Seat** from the copyright holder before use, distribution, or modification.

The **academic citation** block in `LICENSE` is a request, not a legal condition on your MIT rights.
